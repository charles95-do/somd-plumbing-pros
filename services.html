Our Plumbing Services

Everything your plumbing system needs — from a quick fix to a full re-pipe. Serving all of Southern Maryland.

Emergency Plumbing Repair

Plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours — and neither do we. Burst pipes, major leaks, sewer backups, and overflowing fixtures repaired fast by a licensed plumber in Southern Maryland.

  • 24/7 emergency response for burst and frozen pipes
  • Sewer backup and sewage overflow cleanup
  • Gas leak detection and emergency shutoff
  • Overflowing toilet and fixture repair
  • Emergency water shutoff valve repair
  • Flood damage mitigation and pipe isolation

Drain Cleaning & Clog Removal

Slow drains, gurgling pipes, and recurring clogs are signs of a bigger problem. We use professional drain cleaning equipment including hydro-jetting to clear even the toughest blockages.

  • Kitchen sink and garbage disposal clogs
  • Bathroom sink, tub, and shower drain cleaning
  • Toilet clogs and stoppages
  • Main sewer line cleaning and hydro-jetting
  • Floor drain and laundry drain clearing
  • Preventive drain maintenance programs

Water Heater Installation & Repair

No hot water? We repair and replace tank and tankless water heaters from all major brands. Same-day service available so you're not stuck with cold showers.

  • Tank water heater installation (40, 50, 75 gallon)
  • Tankless water heater installation and repair
  • Water heater thermostat and element replacement
  • Anode rod replacement and tank flushing
  • Gas and electric water heater service
  • Water heater expansion tank installation

Sewer Line Repair & Replacement

Tree roots, collapsed pipes, and aging sewer lines are common in Southern Maryland homes. We offer camera inspection, trenchless repair, and full sewer line replacement.

  • Sewer camera inspection and video diagnosis
  • Trenchless sewer line repair (pipe lining/bursting)
  • Traditional sewer line excavation and replacement
  • Tree root removal from sewer lines
  • Sewer cleanout installation
  • Septic-to-sewer connection
